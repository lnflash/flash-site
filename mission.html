Our Mission in the Caribbean

Flash was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to connect the Caribbean through accessible, affordable Bitcoin solutions. In a region where traditional financial services can be expensive and restrictive, we're building a more inclusive financial ecosystem that empowers individuals and businesses alike.

As Caribbean people ourselves, we understand the unique challenges faced by our communities. High remittance fees, limited banking access, and difficulties participating in the global digital economy have held back our region's potential for too long.

Follow Our Journey

2020

The Spark

The Caribbean was overlooked by fintech innovation. We saw excessive remittance fees, businesses struggling with digital payments, and communities left behind in the global economy.

2021

The Vision Takes Shape

El Salvador showed us a future where anyone with a smartphone has banking power without traditional accounts. Bitcoin and Lightning Network became our foundation for instant, low-cost cross-border transactions.

2022

Building the Foundation

We assembled a team of passionate technologists and financial experts. Focused on open-source technology, we launched in Jamaica with simplicity and accessibility as our core principles.

2024

Finding Our Community

Flash resonated in Jamaican communities. From business owners to students, people discovered Bitcoin solved real daily problems. Our growing community provided invaluable feedback to improve the platform.

2025

Expanding Our Impact

Today, Flash connects hundreds of Jamaican residents and businesses. We've built merchant partnerships, created educational resources, and developed rewards programs solving real financial challenges.

Future

The Road Ahead

We're working to connect every Caribbean island, bringing financial inclusion to underserved communities and empowering the next generation to participate fully in the global digital economy.

The Three Pillars of Our Mission

These fundamental tenets of freedom guide everything we do at Flash.

Free the Code

We are committed to open-source development, creating transparent, accessible technology that anyone can verify, contribute to, and build upon. By developing in the open, we ensure our solutions remain accountable to the communities we serve while fostering innovation that benefits everyone, not just those with privileged access.

Free the Money

Financial freedom means access to borderless, affordable, instant value transfer without unnecessary gatekeepers. We're building infrastructure that enables Caribbean people to send, receive, and store value without the prohibitive fees and delays of traditional systems. This economic empowerment is essential for true financial inclusion.

Free the Market

We envision truly open peer-to-peer digital economies where Caribbean businesses and individuals can participate in global commerce without artificial barriers. By promoting decentralized marketplaces and direct economic relationships, we're creating opportunities for growth and prosperity that transcend traditional limitations.

Through financial inclusion and technological innovation, we're building a more prosperous and connected Caribbean.

Our Impact in the Caribbean

Rewarding Communities

Local businesses are the backbone of Caribbean economies, yet they often struggle to compete with foreign vendors. Our FlashCard rewards program enables merchants to thank loyal customers for shopping locally, creating meaningful bonds between businesses and their communities. This strengthens local economies, reduces dependence on outside services, and keeps wealth circulating within Caribbean communities.

Supporting Local Businesses

Caribbean businesses face challenges accepting digital payments and connecting to the global economy. Flash provides merchants with simple tools to accept payments, reach new customers, and grow their businesses beyond traditional borders.

Building Financial Resilience

In regions vulnerable to natural disasters and economic volatility, Flash provides a resilient financial infrastructure that works even when traditional systems are down, helping communities recover faster and build long-term wealth.
