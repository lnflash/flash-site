Frequently Asked Questions

Flash is a secure and easy-to-use app that lets you to receive, save, and spend digital cash instantly and globally. It's designed for consumers and businesses, enabling everyone to participate in the global economy.

Flash is available on iOS, Android, and as an APK file. You can also access it as a Progressive Web Application (PWA) through your web browser.

Visit our Download section and click on the link specific to your device.

Flash uses ECDSA cryptograpy, PIN codes, and biometric authentication (as available on your device) to keep your cash safe. Flash also runs a federation using multi-signature addresses to ensure that your cash is safe and secure, as no single custodian can access your funds. However as the saying goes, “not your keys, not your coins”.

For maximum security, we recommend that you store your cash in a self-custody wallet, and only you know the private key or seed phrase.

To add funds to Flash, you have multiple options:

  • Deposit physical dollars (or whatever medium of exchange is accepted) at one of the many Flash cash-in/cash-out locations.
  • Jump in our peer-to-peer chatrooms and connect with a another Flash user willing send you digital cash exchange for dollars.
  • Receive Bitcoin directly to Flash from any other Lightning or Bitcoin application, from anywhere in the world. By default, Bitcoin is immediately converted to digital cash in Flash.

You have full control over your cash, so at any point you can choose to convert your digital cash in Flash to physical cash. You have multiple options:

  • Withdraw phyisical dollars from one of the many Flash cash-in/cash-out locations.
  • Jump in our peer-to-peer chatrooms and connect with a another Flash user willing to give you physical dollars for your digital cash.
An increasing number of businesss accept Flash, so consider using Flash instead of paying transaction fees to convert to physical dollars.

Transaction fees vary depending on the type of transaction:

  • Flash to Flash: FREE
  • Flash to another Lightning app: 0.01%
  • Flash to a Bitcoin address: 0.1%
  • Flash to a bank account: 2.5%
Cash-in/cash-out locations are considered Flash to Flash, but an additional fee may be assessed by Flash business location.

Anyone can use Flash to accept Bitcoin and Lightning payments by simply displaying a QR code when accepting payment. Businesses can use this QR code to accept payments directly, or they can use the Flash Pay Point-of-Sale (POS) system to accept payments.

To use Flash Pay, you enter the amount that you want to receive, and a QR code will be generated. Then the customer scans the QR code with Flash, and the payment is sent instantly to your Flash. The screen will show a confirmation that the payment was received, and the customer will receive a notification that the payment was sent. Your customers can use any Bitcoin or Lightning app to pay you, and they can pay from anywhere in the world.

The Flash POS webapp is also available for free to accept payments, and can be used on any device with a web browser. The Flash POS app can be found here.

Flash Business allows you to earn additional revenue by providing cash-in and cash-out services to your customers. With Flash Business, you can earn a small fee on every cash-in or cash-out transaction, and your earnings can be withdrawn to your bank account at any time, or kept on Flash for spending. With Flash Business, you will be listed on the Flash Map for free to promote your business to Flash users.

While traditional bank accounts and Flash share some similarities, there are several key differences.

  • Flash is global, so you can send and receive cash from anywhere in the world, instantly.
  • Flash is cryptographically secure, making it virtually impossible for anyone to access your cash without your permission.
  • Flash is more private, as you don’t need to provide any personal information to use it.
Its important to note that with freedom comes responsibility. All cash you store on Flash is your responsibility, so make sure to keep your device PIN secure. Cash stored on Flash is not insured by the FDIC or other governmental insurance bodies.

Treat Flash like the cash in your pocket; we recommend that you only store as much cash on Flash as you would carry in your pocket. Larger amounts of cash should be stored in a self-custody wallet, and only you should know the private key or seed phrase.

Flash is a custodial wallet, so your backups are automatically encrypted and stored. In order to restore your wallet in the event that you lose your device, you will need to follow our restore process and provide your backup phrase. We recommend writing down your backup phrase and storing it in a secure place as soon as possible.
