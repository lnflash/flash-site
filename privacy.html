We may collect various types of information from or about our users, such as:

These technologies help us recognize you and customize your experience, enhance security, measure performance, and analyze traffic patterns. For more information about cookies and how to manage them, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

Purposes of Processing Personal Data

We may process your Personal Data for several purposes, including:

  1. Providing and improving our Services: To deliver the products and services you request, personalize your experience, and ensure the proper functioning of our Services.
  2. Communication: To communicate with you via email, push notifications, SMS messages, or other communication methods, providing updates, promotional offers, and essential service announcements.
  3. Customer Support: To respond to your questions, concerns, or feedback, and resolve technical issues or bugs affecting our Services.
  4. Analytics and Research: To better understand how users engage with our Services, conduct research and analysis, and develop new features and functionalities.
  5. Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, court orders, government requests, and industry standards.
  6. Enforcement: To protect our rights, property, or safety, and those of our users, partners, and affiliates.
  7. Aggregated Insights: To create anonymous, aggregated insights about the demographics, location, behavior, and preferences of our users, which we may share with third parties for marketing, analytics, or research purposes.

Sharing Your Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, lease, or otherwise disclose your Personal Data to third parties except under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your Personal Data with trusted third-party companies who assist us in providing and maintaining our Services, such as cloud hosting providers, payment processors, and customer relationship management tools.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, reorganization, or sale of assets, your Personal Data may be transferred to another company.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your Personal Data if required by law enforcement, courts, regulatory agencies, or other public authorities, or if necessary to enforce our Terms of Service, protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users, partners, and affiliates.

International Transfer of Personal Data

As our operations span multiple countries, your Personal Data may be processed, stored, and accessed in various locations around the world, including outside the Caribbean region. We take appropriate measures to safeguard your Personal Data during international transfers, ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Security Measures

We implement reasonable physical, administrative, and technical safeguards designed to protect your Personal Data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, destruction, or disclosure. Retention Period

We retain your Personal Data for as long as needed to provide our Services, fulfill our contractual obligations, meet legal and regulatory requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Once your Personal Data is no longer necessary for these purposes, it will be deleted or anonymized.

Our Services are not directed towards children under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from individuals under the age of 18 without obtaining verifiable parental consent. If you believe that we have collected Personal Data from a child under the age of 18 without proper consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@getflash.io, and we will promptly delete their information.

  • Restriction: You can ask us to restrict the processing of your Personal Data under specific conditions.
  • Objection: You can object to our processing of your Personal Data based on legitimate interests or the performance of a task in the public interest/exercise of official authority (including profiling).
