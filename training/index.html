📋 Training Overview

This comprehensive training session focused on enhancing the skills of the sales and support teams, addressing both technical issues and the introduction of a new sales platform. The session was divided into two parts covering the new sales platform features and Flash app functionality.

Part 1: Sales Platform

  • • New canvassing & intake forms
  • • Submission management system
  • • Lead tracking & filters
  • • Territory management

Part 2: Flash App

  • • Login methods & security
  • • Flash Wallet functionality
  • • Point-of-sale device features
  • • Payment flow processes

1. Attendance and Technical Issues

  • Recording difficulties were encountered and need to be resolved for future sessions.
  • Technical challenges highlighted the need for better meeting logistics preparation.

2. Introduction to New Sales Platform

  • New platform will replace the old intake form with enhanced functionality.
  • System designed to track merchant visits, conversations, and conversions effectively.
  • Includes canvassing form and intake form for detailed merchant interaction capture.
  • Performance metrics available to track sales conversions and team effectiveness.
  • Support staff can assist salespeople with platform-related issues.

3. Submission Management and User Access

  • Emphasized editing submissions under original owner's account to prevent duplication.
  • Alana can access and edit submissions made by Tatiana for continuity.
  • Territory-based submission lists planned for better organization.
  • Submission list filters crucial for tracking merchant interactions by interest level.
  • Some filter issues acknowledged and will be addressed in future updates.

4. Profile Setup and Territory Management

  • Alana reported issues with changing territory in profile section.
  • Territory editing option not available despite multiple attempts.
  • Issue confirmed and will be addressed post-session.
  • Various tools available for managing lead pipelines and personal profiles.

5. Flash App Login Methods & Security

  • Three login methods: phone number, email backup, and QR code transfer.
  • QR code login allows seamless account migration between devices.
  • Two-factor authentication and PIN codes enhance security measures.
  • Account migration process demonstrated with emphasis on logging out old devices.
  • Security protocols ensure funds only accessible on authorized devices.

6. Flash Wallet Functionality

  • Wallet enables sending and receiving Bitcoin between users.
  • Current manual top-up process involves contacting support team.
  • Automation of wallet top-up process planned for future implementation.
  • No transaction fees for transfers between Flash Wallets.
  • Support team (Ajene, Kimarley, Liz) crucial for smooth user experience.

7. Flashpoint Point-of-Sale Device

  • Device supports QR code payments and tap-to-pay with flashcards.
  • Merchants can purchase with 90-day payment plan option.
  • Quick cash-out process allows fund transfers to bank accounts.
  • Regional coordinators will receive sample devices for demonstrations.
  • Basic functionality designed for merchant ease of use.

8. Data Security & Platform Improvements

  • Chalondra reported difficulties with submission process.
  • Income-related questions to be made optional based on feedback.
  • Kimarley emphasized need for secure logins to protect sensitive data.
  • PIN implementation planned for enhanced platform security.
  • Ongoing feedback encouraged to improve user interface and messaging.

9. Credit Union Integration & Banking

  • Charmaine raised challenges with merchants using credit unions.
  • Credit union transfers depend on RTGS network participation.
  • Most credit unions not directly governed by Bank of Jamaica.
  • Different processes required for credit union fund transfers.
  • Goal remains seamless transactions across all account types.
