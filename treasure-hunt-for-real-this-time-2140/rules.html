The Rules

Welcome to Keys of the Caribbean. These are not suggestions.

01
You DO talk about Keys of the Caribbean.
In fact, you're encouraged to. Brag about your progress. Share your victories. Form alliances. The hunt is better when everyone knows about it.
02
You DO talk about Keys of the Caribbean.
Seriously. Tell your friends. Tweet about it. Make TikToks. The more hunters, the more legendary the hunt becomes.
03
One hunter, one account. No exceptions.
Create multiple accounts and you're out. Forever. We're watching. We have logs. We have patterns. Don't test us.
04
The hunt only ends when you give up.
There's no time limit. Stages remain open until you complete them or until you walk away. Some hunts take hours. Some take weeks. Persistence is rewarded.
05
One stage at a time. No skipping.
You must complete each stage in order. Stage 1 unlocks Stage 2. Stage 2 unlocks Stage 3. There are no shortcuts in the hunt. Progression is earned.
06
No shirts, no shoes, no problem. Just bring your brain.
Physical stages require you to show up in person. But we don't care what you wear. Bring water. Bring sunscreen. Bring your thinking cap. Comfort is your responsibility.
07
The hunt goes on as long as it has to.
No hunter left behind. Whether it takes you three days or three months, your journey matters. The Hall of Heroes has room for everyone who finishes.
08
If this is your first hunt, you HAVE to register.
Choose a username. Provide an email or phone number. That's it. You can be pseudonymous. We don't need your government name. We just need a way to reach you. Prove you know Satoshi. Then the real adventure begins.
09
Cheating is detected. Cheaters are eliminated. Permanently.
We have systems. We have logs. We have patterns. Try to game it and you lose everything—your progress, your prizes, your reputation. Don't be that person.
10
Help others, but don't do it for them.
Share hints. Form teams. Collaborate. But completing stages for someone else? That's against the spirit. Every hunter must solve their own puzzles.
11
Bitcoin prizes go directly to YOUR wallet. Not your keys, not your sats.
We use LNURL-withdraw. When you complete a stage, you get a QR code. Scan it with your Lightning wallet. The sats are yours immediately. No middleman. No custody. Pure sovereignty.
12
Respect the island. Respect the merchants. Respect the hunt.
Physical stages take you to real locations and real businesses. Don't be disruptive. Don't damage property. Don't harass staff. Represent the Bitcoin community well.

⚡ The Real Rule ⚡

Keys of the Caribbean isn't just a treasure hunt. It's a test of character, knowledge, and persistence. It's a journey through Bitcoin's philosophy, Jamaica's culture, and your own limits.

The sats are real. The stages are real. The adventure is real.

Are you ready?
